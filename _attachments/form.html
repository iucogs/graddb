Annual Student Progress Report

To assist the HPS faculty in assessing your progress in the Ph.D. Program, you are required complete an Annual Student Progress Report. Please fill out all of the following categories for which you have activities or achievements to report and submit the completed report and all supporting documentation to the Director of Graduate Studies by May 1st of the appropriate year. Failure to submit a completed progress report and/or the required documentation by the due date will result in an automatic decision of 'continue with warning' by the DGS. It is your responsibility to meet with the members of your Advisory/Research Committee at least one week before the due date to discuss the content of the report. You must also obtain the signatures of all members of your Advisory/Research Committee.
